About us

We are excited to introduce our Coffee House. You’ll love knowing that we provide a unique coffee house environment unlike any other in our city. We are not only a place to drop in and get your morning cup of coffee (althought you more then welcome to do that), we are a place where you can sit down and enjoy that tailor-made cup of coffee. If you need to work, we have a seating area created specifically for you. If you need to rest, we have a soft-seating area in front of a stone fire place that is perfect for your weary mind and body.

We offer a delicious veriety of coffee from Coffee House made by our professionally trained baristas. They know that great espresso takes more than pushing a button. We work hard to deliver great taste while also providing knowledgeable service, perfect if you just want to try something new.

Making our guests smile is our passion, cherishing moments and creating a community where people can come and enjoy a cup of coffee has always been part of our dream...

We look forward to serving you at our Coffee House!
